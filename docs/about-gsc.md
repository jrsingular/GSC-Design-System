# About Global Sports Corporation

**Global Sports Corporation (GSC)** — _Live the dream._

Global Sports Corporation is a Guatemala-based sports company that develops
talent, stages events, and manages sports rights across Central America and
beyond. Founded in **2013**, GSC's mission is to **create opportunities and
transform lives through sport** — building experiences that inspire and empower
its athletes, clients, and community.

## What GSC does

GSC is an international sports organization spanning several complementary lines
of business:

- **Youth development & academies** — recurring training programs for thousands
  of young athletes. GSC has been the **official Juventus Academy partner in the
  region since 2013**, running programs for players roughly **ages 3–18**.
- **Sports events** — organizing and operating competitions, camps, and
  showcase events.
- **Sports marketing & rights management** — representing and commercialising
  sports properties and rights.
- **Talent identification & pathways** — connecting local talent to elite
  international programs.

The work spans multiple disciplines — **football (soccer), basketball, tennis,
and volleyball** — with **football at the core**.

## Reach

- **Headquarters:** Guatemala City, Guatemala.
- **Regional footprint:** Central America, with academy operations reported in
  the **United States** (e.g. Rochester NY, Decatur IL, Boston MA) and **Mexico**
  (Querétaro).
- **Community:** thousands of active athletes in weekly, recurring programs.

## Selected partnerships & programs

- **Juventus Academy** — official regional partner since 2013.
- **LaLiga "Next Gen"** — youth-football development initiative brought to
  Guatemala with GSC.
- **EA SPORTS FC Futures** — global talent-development social project, with
  Guatemala selected as one of a small number of host cities alongside GSC.
- **Investment & commercial initiatives** — GSC also develops investment and
  partnership opportunities in football (for example, club investment
  prospectuses prepared for institutional audiences).

## The brand in one line

Heritage and discipline, in service of opportunity: GSC brings an
institutional, investment-grade standard to sport in a region on the rise —
and invites its audiences to _live the dream._

## Why this matters for the design system

This design system is the visual and verbal identity that carries GSC across
all of it — academy and event communications, marketing and campaign pages,
rights and partnership decks, and investor-facing prospectuses. The
"investment-grade" tone of the system reflects the standard GSC holds itself to
everywhere, not just in finance materials.
