## Voice definition — the four dials

Borrowing NN/g's tone dimensions, GSC sits at fixed points:

| Dial | GSC position | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Funny ⟷ Serious | **Serious** (with dry restraint) | Confidence, not jokes. A wry line is fine; a pun is not. |
| Formal ⟷ Casual | **Lightly formal** | Boardroom-literate but human. Contractions OK. No slang. |
| Irreverent ⟷ Respectful | **Respectful** | Respect the reader's intelligence and the game's history. |
| Enthusiastic ⟷ Matter-of-fact | **Matter-of-fact** | Let the facts carry the excitement. |

GSC is the **"disciplined operator who has already done the work"** — the
equivalent of Mailchimp's "experienced, compassionate partner," but for
institutional sport. Calm authority, not cheerleading.

## Tone by scenario

**Marketing hero.** Boldest register. One claim, one accent word. Permission to
be declarative and a little cinematic — but still true.
> Own the game. / A crown no one else wears. / The window is open.

**Analysis & prospectus body.** Sober and evidenced. Lead with the number, then
the implication. No adjectives doing a number's job.
> Priced at 1.4× revenue — below the 2.7× league median — despite holding more
> titles than any peer.

**Product / app UI.** Quiet and functional. The interface is a tool; copy gets
out of the way. Labels are nouns, actions are verbs.

**Errors & problems.** Calm, plain, no blame, always a next step. Never expose
codes or blame the user.
> That email didn't go through. Check the address and try again.

**Success & confirmation.** Understated. GSC doesn't over-celebrate.
> You're on the list. We'll be in touch.

**Legal & risk.** Precise, complete, unembellished. Never softened into
marketing. Present wherever an investment claim appears.
> This is not an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security. Football
> investment involves risk, including the loss of capital.

## GSC sounds like

- "Priced below its history."
- "Three million fans already live in the United States."
- "Renegotiated media rights. A direct-to-diaspora tier. Recurring revenue."

## GSC never sounds like

- "We're SUPER excited to leverage world-class synergies!!!"
- "A game-changing, revolutionary opportunity you won't want to miss."
- "Unlock unparalleled value in the disruptive sports-tech space."
