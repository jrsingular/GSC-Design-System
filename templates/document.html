Football Investment Opportunity

Comunicaciones FC

A crown no one else wears — the most decorated club in the country, priced below its history, in the strongest economy in Central America.

The asset

Comunicaciones is the flagship of its national league: a record trophy haul, the region's defining derby, and a diaspora of three million fans already living in the United States — a broadcast audience that has never been properly monetised. The club combines the two things institutional capital looks for and rarely finds together: heritage that cannot be manufactured and revenue lines that have barely been touched.

“The league is professionalising in real time. Valuations sit below historical peaks while media rights remain uncommitted — an entry window that does not stay open.”

Where value is made

  • Media rights. Renegotiated distribution plus a direct-to-diaspora streaming tier convert fandom abroad into recurring revenue.
  • Academy pipeline. A player-trading model that turns youth development into a repeatable export business.
  • Matchday & retail. Modern ticketing, hospitality and merchandise, run to an institutional standard.
